Our Story

In 1971, Big Wheel Bikes Opened for business at 33rd and M Streets in Georgetown, next to the C&0 Canal. The big bike on the yellow wall is a Washington, DC landmark. Through eight presidents and 43 years, we've maintained our tradition of selling high quality bikes at discount prices to riders of all skill levels.

Our largest store is in Arlington, VA. off the Spout Run exit of the GW Parkway. Each of our four stores is next to a bike path and offers bike rentals. At every Big Wheel Location, you'll find straight talk, courteous service, and an ability to relate to your specific cycling needs, whether you're a beginner or trathlete. Please visit our stores and eperience first hand the Big Wheel touch.
